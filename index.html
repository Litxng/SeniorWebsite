Street Taco

The History of Tacos

The origin of tacos are unknown in actuality. However, there are theories that they date from the 18th century and in the silver mines in Mexico since in those mines the word "taco: referred to the little charges they would use to excavate the ore. The first references to tacos in any sort of archive or dictionary come from the end of the 19th century. And one of the first types of tacos described is called tacos de minero: miner's taco. In conclusion, although there is no clear origin of the taco, it is considered a traditional mexican dish.
